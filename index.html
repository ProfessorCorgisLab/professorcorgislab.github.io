Frequently Asked

Not here? Email the lab directly.

Are these products for human use? ▼
No. All products sold by Professor Corgi's Laboratory are strictly for laboratory research purposes only. Not intended for human or veterinary use, consumption, or clinical application.
How do I pay? ▼
Payment instructions are sent privately via your order confirmation email after your request is received. Do not send payment until you have your invoice. The confirmation will include the payment method, handle, amount, and memo instructions. Follow them exactly — incorrect payment details result in immediate cancellation.
How does shipping work? ▼
Orders ship via UPS at a flat rate of $10. Orders totaling $120 or more qualify for free shipping. Most orders process and ship within 1–2 business days after payment confirmation. Additional insurance is available on request.
Can I order larger quantities? ▼
Yes. The order form handles standard quantities. For larger or custom orders, contact Professor Corgi directly at ProfessorCorgisLab@gmail.com to discuss availability and pricing.
What's in the Pup Packs? ▼
New Pup Pack ($10): 1x BAC Water 3ml, 1x 0.5ml syringe 10-pack, 20x prep pads.

Pro Pup Pack ($15): 1x BAC Water 10ml, 1x 1.0ml syringe 10-pack, 20x prep pads. (Currently sold out — check back soon.)
What are lab hours? ▼
Mon–Fri, 10am–7pm CST. Orders submitted outside these hours are reviewed the next business day. For urgent matters, email ProfessorCorgisLab@gmail.com.
How will I get my confirmation? ▼
After submitting your order request, Professor Corgi will send a confirmation email to the address you provided. This will include your full payment instructions, invoice, and order details. Check your spam folder just in case.
What if I mess up the payment memo? ▼
Incorrect memo or wrong payment method results in immediate order cancellation and a ban from future orders. Re-read the instructions in your confirmation email before sending. When in doubt, only put your last name and/or email in the memo — nothing else.
