Orders ship via UPS at a flat rate of $10. Orders totaling $120 or more qualify for free shipping. Most orders process and ship within 1–2 business days after payment confirmation. Additional insurance is available on request.

How will I get my confirmation? ▼
After submitting your order request, Professor Corgi will send a confirmation email to the address you provided. This will include Cash App payment instructions and order details. Check your spam folder just in case.
What if I mess up the payment memo? ▼
Incorrect memo or wrong payment method results in immediate order cancellation and a ban from future orders. Re-read the instructions in your confirmation email before sending. When in doubt, only put your last name and/or email in the memo — nothing else.
